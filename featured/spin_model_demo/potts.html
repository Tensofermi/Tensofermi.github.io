Monte Carlo simulation of the Potts model

A multi-state extension of the Ising model, useful for comparing how symmetry and external fields reshape domain formation.

Controls

Explanation

Each color corresponds to one of the \(Q\) local spin states. When \(Q\) changes, both the size of ordered regions and the character of the transition change immediately.

The temperature is measured relative to \(T_c\), while the magnetic field biases the system toward one preferred state. This makes the competition between thermal disorder and external bias easy to inspect visually.
