Monte Carlo simulation of the Ising model

A minimal two-state spin system shown in a compact lattice view. The transition region becomes visually obvious near the critical temperature.

Controls

Explanation

Each cell represents a microscopic magnetic moment. Purple cells indicate spins pointing up, while white cells indicate spins pointing down.

When the temperature approaches the critical value \(T_c \simeq 2.27\), large correlated domains begin to emerge and disappear, making the phase transition easy to observe directly.
